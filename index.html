A really bad telephone number selector form

This is a project I tried to make back in the summer of 2019. The original intention was to have a webpage with a list of every single phone number in a random order, and at the time I tried to do it client side in JavaScript, but it would crash the browser whenever you tried to load it.

My next plan was to attempt to generate it with Liquid, but that didn't work either - the compiler got to 10GB of RAM usage before giving up at the number 268435456 (I'm aware this isn't a valid number but I was testing it). Eventually I decided on a different solution, as inspired by The Library of Babel, where not all of the numbers are displayed at once.

This site was inspired by r/baduibattles, a subreddit where people show off their intentionally bad UIs. Since posting there, I have added the following features as suggested by people in the comments.

  • Randomised order
    • u/JaydeChromium and TheGooseLord both suggested this, although I had actually already planned it. I also set the font to comic sans just to annoy people.
  • Yellow font
    • As suggested by u/0002nam-ytlaS
  • Enter your number again to verify
    • As suggested by u/P-W-L
